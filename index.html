About Me

Programming is, according to me, the perfect mix between problem solving and artistic creativity. This makes it, for a person such as myself, on the border between the aesthetician and the analyst, a perfect activity. Ever since I with the help of Python tried (sometimes successfully) to solve Project Euler problems and constructing simple platformers with my cousins in Java, coding has been one of my great interests. To me the specific programming language is merely a tool serving an inspiring idea, which has resulted my having both a broad insight in different programming paradigms and a capability to rather quickly get a hang of new code bases.

My stengths mainly consists of front-end/fullstack development with the React framework, Sass and Node.js, as well as a variety of applications with Python. I also have a fair amount of experience working with Java and, thanks to my university studies, C# and SQL.

Other than programming, my interests are literature and philosophy, mathematics, painting and fiddling with the 3D animation software Blender. I am twenty-three years old and currently based in Stockholm, Sweden.
